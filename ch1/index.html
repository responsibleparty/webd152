My Favorite Drinks

Intro

Hello, my name is Cecilia and my favorite drink is tea. I like teas of all kinds, especially assam black tea. Assam tea is very versatile and delicious, you can have it by itself or add fruit for a nice summery drink. Teas can be bitter, depending on how long you brew it for, the taste can become bitter and tart the longer you let it steep.
